---
title: "Quick Start"
description: "Get up and running with your first AI-powered insight in minutes."
---

<Steps>

  <Step title="Connect Your Data">
    Go to **Data Hub → Data Sources**.

    Supported sources include:
    - Snowflake
    - BigQuery
    - PostgreSQL
    - CSV / Excel uploads

    Select your source and provide the necessary connection credentials.
    <Warning>Ensure that the service account you provide has read-only access to prevent accidental data modification.</Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore Your Data">
    Navigate to **Data Hub → Table Explorer**. 
    
    Here you can:
    - View all available tables
    - Preview sample data
    - Understand your underlying schema automatically inferred by Prov AI
  </Step>

  <Step title="Define Basic Metrics (Recommended)">
    Go to **Semantic Modeling**. 
    
    Establish your ground truth to ensure consistent and accurate insights:
    - `Revenue = Price × Quantity`
    - Date hierarchy: `Year → Month → Day`
    
    <Info>
      Defining a semantic layer guarantees that the AI will always calculate metrics exactly the way your business does.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Your First Question">
    Go to **Home / AI Analyst** and type a natural language prompt:
    
    > *"Total sales last month by region"*
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Results">
    The system will immediately:
    - Generate the exact SQL
    - Query your data warehouse
    - Display an auto-selected optimal visualization (e.g. Bar Chart)

    You can manually switch the chart type, apply filters, and view the generated SQL for full transparency.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Follow-Up Questions">
    You don't need to write the entire query again. Just ask a follow-up!
    
    > *"Break this down by product category"*
    
    <Tip>The Prov AI system maintains conversation context, automatically updating the SQL and results based on the previous context.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save or Share">
    Once you find an interesting insight, you can:
    - **Save** as a dashboard widget
    - **Export** as a PDF/CSV report
    - **Share** with your team via a secure link
    - **Schedule** recurring email/Slack reports
  </Step>

</Steps>

---

## 🎉 You're Done!

You’ve successfully connected data, queried it using conversational AI, and generated actionable insights. Next step: explore advanced features like custom dashboard generation.
