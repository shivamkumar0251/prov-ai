**Calculated Fields** allow you to create new, virtual data columns on top of your existing raw tables using custom formulas. They empower you to inject specific business logic directly into the semantic model, ensuring the AI query engine calculates things perfectly every time.

---

## Why Use Calculated Fields?

Often, the data stored in a warehouse isn't exactly what users want to query. For instance, you might have `sales` and `costs`, but users ask for `profit margin`.

Instead of making users calculate this manually in Excel, you define it once as a Calculated Field in Prov AI.

## Example Formulas

Here is how you might map standard fields into calculated measures:

```text
Revenue       = Price × Quantity
Net Profit    = Revenue - Total_Costs
Profit Margin = (Net Profit / Revenue) × 100
```

<Tip>
Calculated fields are executed natively on your data warehouse. We compile the formula directly into the highly-optimized SQL query that runs on Snowflake, BigQuery, or Postgres.
</Tip>
