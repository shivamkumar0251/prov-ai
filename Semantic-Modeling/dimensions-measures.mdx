In data analytics, every piece of information is structured into two fundamental categories: **Dimensions** and **Measures**. Correctly classifying these fields in Prov AI allows the AI engine to generate accurate charts and SQL groupings.

---

## Dimensions (The 'What')

Dimensions are descriptive attributes of your data. They provide the context, giving you the ability to "slice and dice" your metrics.

**Used to:** Group data, filter results, and define the X-axis of your charts.

## Measures (The 'How Much')

Measures are the quantitative numeric values used for mathematical calculations and aggregations (SUM, AVG, COUNT, MIN, MAX).

**Used to:** Quantify performance, calculate trends, and define the Y-axis of your charts.

## How They Work Together

When a user asks:
> *"Show me Revenue by Region"*

The AI Engine instantly knows how to formulate the SQL:

```sql
SELECT 
    Region,          -- Dimension (Used for GROUP BY)
    SUM(Revenue)     -- Measure (Aggregated to show total)
FROM sales_data
GROUP BY Region;
```
