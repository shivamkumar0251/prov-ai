**Conversational Analytics** shifts the paradigm from isolated dashboard filters to a continuous, intelligent conversation. Prov AI remembers the context of the thread, so you never have to repeat yourself.

<Tip>
  Think of the AI Analyst like a human data colleague. Once you establish the topic, you can just ask it to pivot, filter, or re-group the data naturally.
</Tip>

---

## Example Flow

Instead of building 3 different charts, you can just converse:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Query">
    **User:** *"Show me sales by region in a bar chart."*<br/>
    *(AI renders a bar chart for all-time sales across all regions.)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by Time">
    **User:** *"Only show for Q4."*<br/>
    *(AI automatically updates the chart to filter by the specified quarter, keeping 'sales' and 'region'.)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine Scope">
    **User:** *"Give me just the top 3 regions."*<br/>
    *(AI applies a \`LIMIT 3\` and \`ORDER BY\` to the existing context.)*
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technical Mechanisms

How Prov AI tracks your conversation under the hood:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Context Awareness" icon="memory">
    Stores previous queries, applied filters, and selected metrics in memory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Query Rewriting" icon="pen-nib">
    The AI dynamically rewrites the new SQL statement by appending the new conditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Semantic Adherence" icon="shield-check">
    Guarantees filters apply to the correct tables as defined in your Semantic Layer.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why it matters

Conversational analytics drastically reduces the time to insight. It eliminates repetitive work, avoids the need to build a new report for every minor variation of a business question, and **makes analytics feel like a conversation, not a tool.**
