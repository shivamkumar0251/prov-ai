**Natural Language Query (NLQ)** enables every business user to interact with complex datasets using everyday language. 

By removing the strict syntax requirements of SQL, Prov AI democratizes access to data while maintaining full technical accuracy under the hood.

---

## How NLQ Works

The pipeline from prompt to execution happens securely in fractions of a second:

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Enters Query">
    Example: *"Show me the total sales in Q4 broken down by region."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intent Parsing">
    The AI identifies the core components:
    - **Metric**: Sales
    - **Dimension**: Region
    - **Filter**: Q4
  </Step>
  <Step title="Entity Mapping">
    The engine checks the Semantic Layer to map these intent signals to physical schema objects (e.g., \`orders.amount\`, \`regions.name\`, \`date >= '2023-10-01'\`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="SQL Generation">
    Prov AI synthesizes this into an optimized query.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execution & Rendering">
    The query is safely executed, and the data is visualized.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Behind the Scenes Example

When you ask Prov AI:
> *"Top 5 products by revenue last month"*

The AI handles the heavy lifting, generating the exact dialect required for your data warehouse:

```sql
SELECT 
    product_name, 
    SUM(revenue) as total_revenue
FROM sales_data
WHERE purchase_date >= DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '1 month')
  AND purchase_date < DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE)
GROUP BY product_name
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC
LIMIT 5;
```

<Note>
The user never has to worry about date truncation formatting, grouping, or aliases. The NLQ engine handles all dialect-specific syntax.
</Note>
