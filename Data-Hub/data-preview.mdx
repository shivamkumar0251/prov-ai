**Data Preview** allows you to see a live sample of your data to help you understand its structure and content before generating insights or building semantic models.

<Info>
Previewing data helps verify that your connector is configured correctly and that the table contains the expected information.
</Info>

---

## What You See

When you trigger a Data Preview, Prov AI securely fetches and displays:
- **Sample rows** (e.g., the first 50 rows)
- **Null value presence** (to identify sparse columns quickly)
- **Data distribution** summary
