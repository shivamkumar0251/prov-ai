Connectors define how the Prov AI platform integrates with your different data systems. They act as the secure bridge between your raw data sources and the AI analytics engine.

---

## Types of Connectors

  <Card title="1. Native Connectors" icon="database">

  <Card title="2. API-Based Connectors" icon="cloud">

---

## Connector Capabilities

All supported connectors provide the following advanced features automatically:
- **Schema Discovery:** Instantly reads and maps tables and columns.
- **Auto-Refresh:** Keeps schema metadata up to date.
- **Authentication Handling:** Securely stores credentials.
- **Query Execution:** Dispatches optimized SQL natively to the source.

---

## Configuration & Data Sync

Each connector requires a standard setup: specific credentials, connection name, database/dataset selection, and sync settings.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Real-time (Live Query)" icon="bolt">
    Queries are executed directly against your database at the exact moment the user asks a question. **Recommended for production.**
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Scheduled Sync" icon="clock">
    Data/metadata is periodically synced (e.g., hourly, daily) into a cache for faster performance on static data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Manual Refresh" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Users trigger a sync manually. Useful for flat file uploads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

<Warning>
**Important Considerations:**
- **Schema changes can break AI queries:** Always re-sync metadata after altering tables in your database.
- **API limitations:** API-based connectors are subject to the rate limits of the external SaaS system.
- **File uploads are static:** CSV/Excel uploads are not real-time. Data must be re-uploaded to reflect updates.
</Warning>

---

## Best Practices

- Use **native connectors** for optimal performance and security.
- Set **refresh schedules** based strictly on business needs to avoid unnecessary load.
- Always **validate data** after the initial sync.
