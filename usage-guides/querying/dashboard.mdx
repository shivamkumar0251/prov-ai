The **Dashboard** feature allows you to pin generated charts for quick access. This ensures that your most important visual data insights are always at your fingertips. 

---

## How to Use the Dashboard

### 1. Generate a Chart
Start by asking a question on the **Home** page. Once the results are generated, click the **Chart** tab to view the visualization.

### 2. Pin to Dashboard
Click the **Pin** icon at the top of any generated chart. You can select an existing dashboard or create a new one.

### 3. Access Pinned Charts
Navigate to the **Dashboard** section in the sidebar to view your pinned insights.

---

## Managing Your Dashboards

- **Switch Dashboards**: Use the sidebar to toggle between multiple dashboards for different projects or teams.
- **Rename or Delete**: Click the **More icon (⋮)** on any dashboard item in the sidebar.
- **Unpin a Chart**: Click the **Delete** option on any pinned chart card.
- **Arrange & Resize**: Click and drag any chart to reorder. Use the bottom-right corner to resize for a custom layout.

<Tip>
  Building themed dashboards (e.g., "Monthly Sales" or "User Engagement") is a great way to keep your team aligned.
</Tip>
