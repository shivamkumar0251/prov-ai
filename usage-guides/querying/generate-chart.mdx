Prov AI automatically generates insightful charts to help you visualize your data and uncover meaningful patterns. 

---

## Automatic Chart Generation

When you ask a question, Prov AI analyzes your data and automatically selects the most appropriate chart type for the results. Click the **Chart** tab in any thread to view the visualization.

---

## Chart Gallery

Prov AI supports a wide variety of chart types powered by **Vega-Lite**:

### Basic Charts
- **Bar Charts**: Compare quantities across categories (Grouped, Stacked, Horizontal).
- **Line Charts**: Ideal for trends and changes over time (Area, Step).
- **Scatter Plots**: Show correlations between variables (Bubble charts).
- **Pie Charts**: Show proportions of a whole.

### Advanced Visualizations
- **Maps**: For geographical data analysis.
- **Mixed Charts**: Combine different types (e.g., Line + Bar).
- **Heat Maps**: Patterns in matrix data.
- **Metrics & KPIs**: Highlight key numbers.

<Note>
  Prov AI is not limited to these types; we support any visualization that can be expressed with Vega-Lite syntax.
</Note>

---

## Adjusting the Chart

You can modify your chart using **natural language**. Simply describe what you want to change:

- **Style**: *"Make the bars blue"* or *"Increase the font size."*
- **Enhancements**: *"Add data labels above each point."*
- **Types**: *"Convert this to a stacked area chart"* or *"Create a dual-axis chart."*

---

## Controls

### Regenerate & Restore
- **Regenerate**: Click the **Regenerate icon** if the initial chart doesn't meet your needs.
- **Restore**: Use the **Restore to last version** button to undo your latest manual adjustment.

### Save & Pin
- **Download**: Export your chart as a **PNG** or **SVG** file.
- **Pin**: Save the chart to your **Dashboard** for persistent access.
