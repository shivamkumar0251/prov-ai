After Prov AI generates an answer, you are redirected to a **Thread Page**. This page contains all the components of your data insight.

---

## Answer Components

### 1. Summarized Answer
A concise, AI-generated summary of the data insights, making key findings easy to understand at a glance.

### 2. Data Preview
The actual results table generated from the SQL query. You can explore the raw data directly below the summary.

### 3. Generate Chart
Click the **Chart** tab to view an automatic visualization of the data. You can further adjust this chart using natural language.

---

## Actions

- **Save as View**: Click **Save as View** at the bottom of any answer to store the result SQL. This is useful for frequently used queries or connecting to external apps.
- **Download Result**: Export the data table as a **CSV** file.

---

## Follow-up Questions

### Recommended Questions
Prov AI provides AI-generated suggestions based on your current results to help you dig deeper into the data.

### Ask Your Own
Type your own follow-up questions in the thread question box. Prov AI maintains context from the previous answers, allowing for a continuous conversation with your data.

<Note>
  Follow-up questions allow you to refine your search (e.g., "Now filter this for the last 30 days") without starting a new project.
</Note>
