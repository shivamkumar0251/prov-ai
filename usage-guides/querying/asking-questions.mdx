On the **Home** page, you can ask questions or data requests and get AI-generated answers from your connected data. Prov AI supports two distinct modes to help you get the most accurate results.

## Choose a Mode

Prov AI offers two primary modes for interacting with your data:

### 1. Ad-hoc Mode (One-shot Execution)
Best for well-defined, straightforward requests. Prov AI generates the SQL and returns results directly without further clarification.

### 2. Interactive Mode (Guided Conversation)
Best for complex queries. Prov AI may ask clarifying questions to confirm your intent before running the final SQL query.

---

## Ad-hoc Mode

Switch to **Ad-hoc Mode** by selecting the tab next to the question box.

### Classification Categories
Prov AI automatically classifies your input into:
- **Data Retrieval Requests**: Clearly defined requests converted into SQL.
- **Database Schema Exploration**: Questions about your table structure or relationships.
- **Out-of-Scope Queries**: Inputs unrelated to your data (e.g., "Hello").

### What to Expect
For every successful data retrieval request, Prov AI provides:
1. A **Concise Summary** of insights.
2. **Data Visualizations**: Automatic charts where applicable.
3. **Full SQL Breakdown**: Deep transparency into the generation process.

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip**: If your question is vague, Prov AI will suggest alternative questions to help refine your intent.
</Tip>

---

## Database Schema Exploration

Inquire about your database structure with natural language:
- *"What tables do I have?"*
- *"Explain the Customer table to me."*
- *"How many orders were placed last month?"*

This helps you understand your data landscape without needing technical documentation.

---

## Interactive Mode

Switch to **Interactive Mode** for a more conversational experience.

### How it Works
1. **Understand Intent**: Prov AI interprets natural language for ranking, trends, or comparisons.
2. **Apply Instructions**: It checks for predefined business rules (e.g., "exclude canceled orders").
3. **Select Models**: The system scans your connected datasets for the most relevant models.
4. **Execute & Preview**: SQL is generated and run securely, allowing you to preview data in real-time.

---

## Advanced Features

### Follow-up Questions
In any thread, you can ask subsequent questions to refine your results. Prov AI maintains context from your previous queries.

### Suggested Instructions
When patterns are detected, Prov AI provides **Suggested Instructions** to help you maintain consistency across your team.
- **Save to Knowledge**: Store instructions for future use.
- **Edit**: Adjust the logic before saving.

<Note>
  Standardizing logic via instructions ensures that everyone in your organization queries data the same way.
</Note>
