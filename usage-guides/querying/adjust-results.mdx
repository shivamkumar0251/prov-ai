Want to add more columns to your results? No problem! Our **Adjust Results** feature lets you customize your answers on the fly. 

After asking a question and viewing the results, simply click the **Adjust Results** button to add new columns. Enter your desired fields, press Enter, and watch your results update in real-time.

---

## How it Works

1. **Ask a Question**: Start by querying your data in the Prov AI Home page.
2. **View Results**: Once the AI generates an answer, you'll see the data table.
3. **Adjust**: Click the **Adjust Results** button located above the table.
4. **Refine**: Add or remove columns to tailor the output to your specific needs.

<Tip>
  This is a great way to explore related metrics without starting a completely new thread.
</Tip>
