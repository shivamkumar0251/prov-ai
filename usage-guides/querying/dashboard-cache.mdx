---

Dashboard caching improves performance by storing the results of your queries. This is particularly useful for dashboards with complex calculations or large datasets that don't change frequently.

---

## Enabling Dashboard Cache

To enable caching for your dashboard:

1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** in the top-right corner of your dashboard.
2. Select **Cache Settings**.
3. Toggle **Enable Cache** to ON.
4. Configure your **Refresh Schedule**:
   - **Daily** or **Weekly**.
   - **Custom Schedule**: Specific times or intervals.
   - **Manual Refresh Only**: Results will only update when you click refresh.

---

## Manual Cache Refresh

You can trigger a manual refresh at two levels:

### For Individual Charts
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** on a specific chart card.
2. Select **Refresh Cache**.

### For the Entire Dashboard
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
2. Select **Refresh All Caches**.

---

## Monitoring Cache Status

To verify if a chart is using cached data:

1. Look at the bottom of the chart card.
2. The **Last Refreshed** timestamp indicates when the data was last pulled from your source.

<Info>
  **Performance Tip**: If you find your dashboard is slow to load, consider enabling cache and setting a daily refresh during off-peak hours.
</Info>
