## Choose Your Data Source Type

To add a new connection:
1. Navigate to the **Connect a data source** section in your project.
2. Click on your specific data source provider.
3. Follow the on-screen prompts to provide credentials and connection strings.

---

## Update Connection

To update an existing data source's properties (such as rotating credentials or updating the host):
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Navigate to the **Data source** tab.
3. Modify the necessary fields and save your changes.

<Note>
  Updating connection properties may trigger a schema re-sync to ensure Prov AI is aware of any structure changes in your database.
</Note>
