## Find Connection Information

Navigate to your Neon Dashboard and click the **Connect** button to view your database credentials.

<Tip>
  We recommend using the **Transaction Pooler** for stable performance with Prov AI Cloud.
</Tip>

---

## Connect

### Example Setup

Based on your Neon dashboard, your connection details will look similar to this:
- **Host**: `[hidden]-pooler.us-east-2.aws.neon.tech`
- **Port**: `5432`
- **Database**: `neondb`
- **Username**: `neondb_owner`
- **SSL Mode**: `require`

### Connection Explained

- **Display Name**: The identifier for this database in Prov AI.
- **Host**: Your Neon Postgres hostname.
- **Port**: The default port is `5432`.
- **Username**: Your Neon database user (default: `neondb_owner`).
- **Password**: The password generated by Neon.
- **Database**: The specific database name (default: `neondb`).

<Note>
  All further steps, such as **Selecting Tables** and **Defining Relationships**, are identical to the standard [PostgreSQL guide](/usage-guides/data-sources/postgresql).
</Note>
