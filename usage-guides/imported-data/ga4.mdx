Analyze your **Google Analytics (GA4)** data in Prov AI Cloud using our boilerplate. This template provides a structured framework for importing and analyzing web engagement, saving you time and effort.

---

## Prerequisites

- **GCP Account & Project ID** with BigQuery enabled.
- **GA4 Account Editor** permissions.
- **BigQuery Dataset** populated with GA4 data.

### Exporting GA4 to BigQuery
1. In your **Google Analytics Project**, go to **Admin > Product Links > BigQuery Links**.
2. Link your BigQuery Project.
3. Select the data streams and events for export.

<Note>
  You must enable the **BigQuery API** in your Google Cloud Developer Console first.
</Note>

---

## Setup Steps

1. Select **Google Analytics** in the "Start with a Data Boilerplate" section.
2. Provide **BigQuery Data Viewer** permissions to the Prov AI service account principal address displayed on your project page.
3. Enter your **GCP Project ID** and **Dataset ID**.
4. Click **Continue**.

<Info>
  The **Viewer** role is read-only. Prov AI will never modify or delete your GCP resources.
</Info>

## Review & Analyze

- **Pre-filled Fields**: The modeling page will display events, orders, traffic sources, and device info.
- **Ask Questions**: Use the thread view to ask about engagement, acquisition, and monetization.

### What is `user_pseudo_id`?
In GA4, this is a unique identifier assigned by Google to track interactions anonymized from PII. You can customize this implementation for more specific tracking needs.
