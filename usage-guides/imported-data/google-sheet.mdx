By connecting your Google Sheets, you can enrich your analysis by combining spreadsheet data with other sources, gaining deeper insights into your operations.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active **Google Account**.
- **Read Access**: Prov AI only requires read access to your specific spreadsheet.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Go to **Settings > Data Connection** and click **Connect a data source**.
2. Select **Google Sheets** from the menu.
3. Enter a **Display Name**.
4. Sign in with your Google account and paste the spreadsheet **URL**.

<Note>
  Currently, the HubSpot boilerplate is required to integrate Google Sheet data sources into your project.
</Note>

---

## Modeling Spreadsheet Data

1. Navigate to the **Modeling** page.
2. Click the **+** button next to **Models**.
3. Select the sheet and columns you wish to analyze.
4. Click **Create** to finalize your model.

<Tip>
  Add detailed descriptions to each column. This helps Prov AI understand the context of your data, leading to more accurate AI analysis.
</Tip>
