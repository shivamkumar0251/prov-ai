Get started with your Facebook Marketing data in Prov AI using our boilerplate. This template provides pre-built models and semantics for analyzing ad performance, campaign effectiveness, and audience insights.

---

## Prerequisites

- **Facebook Ad Account ID**.
- **Permissions**: You must be an Admin of the Ad account or have explicitly granted permissions to access it.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Select **Facebook Marketing** in the "Start with a data boilerplate" section.
2. Provide your **Facebook Account ID**.
3. Select a **Start Date** (and optional End Date) for data synchronization.
4. Click **Authenticate your Facebook account**.

<Info>
  **Integration Partner**: We partner with **Airbyte** to provide a seamless import process. You may see the Airbyte logo during the authentication step.
</Tip>

---
