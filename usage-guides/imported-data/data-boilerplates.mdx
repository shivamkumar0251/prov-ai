Boilerplates are pre-defined templates that offer pre-selected tables and columns, eliminating the need to import fields manually. They also include curated questions to help you find valuable insights quickly.

## Benefits

- **Save Time**: Avoid the hassle of manual schema mapping.
- **Consistency**: Maintain a standardized structure for common analytics use cases.
- **Focus on Insights**: Start asking questions immediately with pre-built models.

---

## Supported Boilerplates

Prov AI currently supports the following legacy boilerplates:

- **HubSpot**: For CRM and marketing automation.
- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)**: For web traffic and event analysis.
- **WooCommerce**: For e-commerce store performance.
- **Facebook Marketing**: For ad campaign tracking.
- **Google Ads**: For search and display ad metrics.
- **Stripe**: For subscription and payment analysis.

---

## How to Use

1. **Select**: Choose the boilerplate that matches your data source during project setup.
2. **Customize**: Add additional columns from your source as needed.
3. **Analyze**: Use the included recommended questions to explore your data.
