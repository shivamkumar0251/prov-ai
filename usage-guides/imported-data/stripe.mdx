Analyze your Stripe payment data in Prov AI Cloud by using our boilerplate. This template provides pre-built models and metrics for analyzing revenue trends and customer behavior.

---

## Prerequisites

- **Stripe Account**.
- **Stripe API Key**: A restricted or secret key with **Read** permissions for the necessary resources.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Select **Stripe** in the "Start with a data boilerplate" section.
2. Enter your **Account ID** and **API Key**.
3. Select a **Start Date** for data synchronization.
4. Click **Start Syncing**.

---

## Review & Analyze

- **Pre-filled Fields**: After syncing, the modeling page will display payment, refund, and customer models.
- **Expand Analysis**: You can add additional sources like MySQL or Google Sheets to enrich your Stripe data.
