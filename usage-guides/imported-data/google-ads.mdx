The Google Ads boilerplate provides pre-configured models and semantics for analyzing ad performance directly in Prov AI Cloud.

---

## Prerequisites

- **GCP BigQuery Dataset** with Google Ads data. Follow the [Google Ads Transfer documentation](https://cloud.google.com/bigquery/docs/google-ads-transfer) to set this up.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Select **Google Ads** in the "Start with a Data Boilerplate" section.
2. Provide **BigQuery Data Viewer** permissions to the Prov AI service account principal address.
3. Enter your **GCP Project ID** and **Dataset ID**.
4. Click **Continue**.

<Info>
  Granting the **Viewer** role allows Prov AI Cloud to explore your schema and generate queries without modifying any data.
</Info>

---

## Review Fields

The modeling page will display all models and metrics from the Google Ads boilerplate. Visual markers help you distinguish between boilerplate models and custom views.
