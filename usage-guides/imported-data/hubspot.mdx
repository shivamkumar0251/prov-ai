Unlock the power of your HubSpot data with our pre-built boilerplate. Gain instant access to essential marketing tables for contacts, companies, email campaigns, deals, and engagements.

---

## HubSpot Support

Currently, Prov AI HubSpot integration supports **marketing-related** tables and objects. Pipelines for tickets and certain sales-specific tables are not yet available.

### Use Cases
- **Unified Analytics**: Combine HubSpot CRM data with product logs in MySQL or feedback in Airtable.
- **Product Marketing Synergy**: Merge feature adoption metrics with contact lists to build personalized retention strategies.
- **Lead Scoring**: Analyze deal flows alongside website traffic data from GA4.

---

## Prerequisites

- **HubSpot Tier**: Starter, Professional, or Enterprise.
- **Permissions**: Super Admin account or custom permissions (View for contacts, companies, deals; View/Edit for tickets).

---

## Setup Steps

1. Select **HubSpot** in the "Start with a data boilerplate" section.
2. Authenticate your account via the HubSpot pop-up.
3. Choose the specific account and grant the requested scopes.
4. Click **Start Syncing**. Sync time varies based on data volume.

---

## Customizing Data

### Adding Custom Properties
1. Select a table in the **Modeling** page.
2. Click the **⋮** menu and select **Update Columns**.
3. Move your custom properties into the model and submit.

### Data Synchronization
Prov AI periodically syncs your data and optimizes structures using LLM. You can adjust the schedule in **Settings > Data Connection**.
