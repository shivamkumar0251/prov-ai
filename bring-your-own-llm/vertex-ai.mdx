---

## Prerequisites

1.  **Enable Vertex AI API**: Go to the Google Cloud Console and enable the Vertex AI API.
2.  **Service Account**: Create a service account with the **Vertex AI User** role.
3.  **JSON Key**:
    - Go to **IAM & Admin > Service Accounts**.
    - Find your service account, go to **Keys**, and select **Add Key > Create New Key (JSON)**.
    - Download and store the key securely.

---

## Setup Steps

### 1. Initial Connection
Select **Quick Setup** and choose **Google Gemini (Vertex AI)**. Enter your **Vertex Location** (Region), **Project ID**, and upload your service account JSON file.

### 2. Test Connection
In the **Embedding Model** section, click **Test Connection** to ensure Prov AI can reach your Vertex AI resource.

### 3. Update Model Configuration
While default configurations are provided, we recommend optimizing your settings:

**Recommended Configuration**:
```json
{
  "kwargs": {
    "n": 1,
    "timeout": 60,
    "temperature": 0
  },
  "context_window_size": 1000000
}
```

### 4. Pipeline Assignment
Review the pipeline assignments. Prov AI provides defaults optimized for Gemini models:
- **Advanced Capabilities**: `gemini-1.5-pro`
- **Standard Tasks**: `gemini-1.5-flash`

Click **Complete Setup** to finalize.

---

<Note>
  Gemini 1.5 models offer large context windows (1M+ tokens), making them ideal for complex Prov AI analysis over large datasets.
</Note>
