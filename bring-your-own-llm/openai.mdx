Prov AI is optimized for OpenAI models, offering a seamless "Quick Setup" experience for getting your analysis project up and running in minutes.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active **OpenAI Account**.
- A valid **API Key**.

---

## Setup Steps

1. **Select Provider**: During onboarding, select **Quick Setup** and choose **OpenAI**.
2. **Enter Key**: Paste your OpenAI API Key and click continue.
3. **Verify Connection**: In the **Embedding Model** section, click **Test Connection** to ensure Prov AI can communicate with your models.
4. **Review Selections**: Prov AI automatically selects production-ready defaults (e.g., `gpt-4o` and `text-embedding-3-small`).
5. **Finalize**: Review the automatic pipeline assignments and click **Complete Setup**.

---

<Tip>
  Prov AI’s default assignments for OpenAI are fine-tuned for the best balance of performance and cost.
</Tip>
