This section provides a categorized list of examples demonstrating how to interact effectively with the AI Analyst. Use these as a starting point for your own data exploration!

---

## Basic Queries

Great for getting immediate, high-level numbers:
- *"Total sales last month"*
- *"Revenue by region"*
- *"Top 5 products by revenue"*

---

## Time-Based Queries

The AI natively understands temporal logic and date math:
- *"Sales in Q4"*
- *"Revenue growth over the last 6 months"*
- *"Show active users year to date (YTD)"*

---

## Comparative Queries

Compare different slices of data effortlessly:
- *"Compare sales between 2023 and 2024"*
- *"Revenue by region vs last quarter"*

---

## Filtered Queries

Apply strict conditions to your analysis:
- *"Sales in the US region only"*
- *"Top products in the electronics category"*

---

## Advanced Queries

Calculate complex ratios and aggregate statistics in plain English:
- *"Customer churn rate by month"*
- *"Average order value by user segment"*

---

<Warning>
**Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**
- **Vague Queries:** Prompting *"How are we doing?"* produces highly generalized results. Be specific!
- **Incorrect Terminology:** Misnaming variables might force the AI to map the term to the wrong database column.
</Warning>
