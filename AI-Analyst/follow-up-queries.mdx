---
title: "Follow-Up Queries"
description: "Refine your data context naturally without starting over."
---

**Follow-up queries** are the cornerstone of conversational analytics. They allow users to progressively refine their results and drill down into data without needing to repeat the full context of their original question.

---

## How it works

Prov AI maintains a "memory" of your current conversational thread. When you ask a follow-up question, the AI intelligently merges your new constraint with the previous SQL filters.

---

## Example Flow

Instead of writing three separate, complex SQL queries, you manage the data exactly how you would in a human conversation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initial Query">
    **User:** *"Sales by region"*  
    **AI:** *(Executes query and renders a Bar Chart comparing regions)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter the Context">
    **User:** *"Only for Q4"*  
    **AI:** *(Rewrites the SQL to include `WHERE quarter = 'Q4'`, updating the chart)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine the View">
    **User:** *"Top 3 regions"*  
    **AI:** *(Adds `LIMIT 3` and updates the final result)*
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you ever want to start a completely new, unrelated analysis, simply start a New Chat or explicitly say *"Clear filters"*. 
</Info>
