## How it Works

### Project Roles
- **Project Owner**: Full control over settings, members, and all project content.
- **Project Contributor**: Can create and edit threads, models, and dashboards.
- **Project Viewer**: View-only access to content and settings.

### Shared Workspace
Organize your threads into folders for better structure. Grant access to folders based on your team's needs while maintaining thread ownership.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Set up Access**: Add team members to your project and assign appropriate roles in **Settings**.
2. **Organize**: Create folders to group related analysis threads (e.g., "Sales Q1", "User Retention").
3. **Share**: Use **Manage Access** to invite specific people or the entire team to your folders.

---

## Best Practices

- **Viewer First**: Start by giving new members limited access, then upgrade as their responsibilities grow.
- **Descriptive Names**: Use clear folder names to help team members understand content at a glance.
- **Audit Access**: Periodically review project roles to ensure data security.
