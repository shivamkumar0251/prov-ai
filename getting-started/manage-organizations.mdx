Once an organization is created, a **default project** is automatically generated for you. This project will always appear at the top of your list.

### Connect an External Data Source

You can connect a wide range of data sources to your project:

- **Cloud Data Warehouses**: BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift, Athena, Databricks.
- **Relational Databases**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle, SQL Server.
- **OLAP & Big Data**: ClickHouse, Trino, Spark (EMR/Athena).
- **Files**: CSV Upload.

## Members & Access Control

The **Members** section lists all users in the organization and their roles. Admins can invite, remove, and modify member roles.

### Invite People
1. Click the **Invite People** button.
2. Enter the user's email and assign a role.
3. **Roles**:
   - **Organization Admin**: Access to all projects and the ability to create new ones.
   - **Organization Member**: Must be added to at least one specific project.

<Tip>
  You can also add invited users to multiple projects simultaneously during the onboarding step.
</Tip>

### Managing Invitations
Once sent, check invitation status and links on the **Members** page. 

<Warning>
  **Expirations**: Invitations expire after **3 days** if not accepted.
</Warning>

<img src="/images/manage-org-6.png" alt="Members Page" />

### Update or Remove Members
- **Update Role**: Use the **Role** dropdown menu. Note: You cannot modify your own role—another Admin must do this for you.
- **Remove Member**: Click **Remove** to revoke all permissions for that user.

## Danger Zone

<Warning>
  **Delete Organization**: This action is **irreversible**. All projects within the organization will be permanently lost for all users.
</Warning>

- **Leave Organization**: Members can leave at any time. If you are the last Admin, you must delete the organization instead.
