**Visualizations** are the fastest way for human brains to process complex datasets. Prov AI transforms your raw query results into highly optimized, interactive charts instantly.

---

## Auto-Visualization Engine

You do not need to manually configure X and Y axes. The system automatically selects the absolute best chart based on:
1. **Data Types:** (e.g., Dates usually imply a Line Chart).
2. **Query Intent:** (e.g., "Compare" implies a Bar Chart).
3. **Dimensionality:** (e.g., Too many groupings will default to a Table).

## Customization Options

If the AI's selection isn't perfectly suited to your needs, you always have full control:
- Instantly switch chart types via a dropdown.
- Adjust axis mappings.
- Apply visual filters and zoom/drill-down into specific segments.

---

## Best Practices

<Warning>
**Visualization Pitfalls:**
- **Misleading Charts:** The wrong chart type can completely misrepresent the data. If comparing parts of a whole, use a Pie Chart; if comparing totals, use a Bar Chart.
- **Clutter:** Too many dimensions (e.g., a pie chart with 40 slices) reduces readability to zero. Let the AI group small values into "Other".
</Warning>

- Keep visual dashboards beautifully simple.
- Prioritize interactive charts where drill-down exploration adds value.
