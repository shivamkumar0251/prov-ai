---
title: "Reports"
description: "Structured, shareable outputs combining data, charts, and explanations."
---

**Reports** enable the easy sharing of insights across teams. While Dashboards are interactive and live, Reports are structured outputs designed for summarizing analysis at a specific point in time.

---

## Types of Reports

- **Static Reports:** A snapshot of data exported at a specific moment.
- **Scheduled Reports:** Automatically generated and emailed (e.g., Daily Sales at 8:00 AM).
- **AI-Generated Reports:** Deep-dive narratives produced entirely by the AI based on a broad topic.

## Best Practices

<Warning>
**Important Considerations:**
- **Data Freshness:** Static reports can quickly become outdated. Always ensure timestamps are clearly visible.
- **Validation:** Always validate the underlying logic before sharing high-stakes reports broadly.
</Warning>

- Schedule reports regularly for consistent cadence.
- Use very clear titles and axis labels.
- Utilize custom templates for branded exports.
