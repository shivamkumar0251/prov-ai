## Managing Your Subscription

### Upgrading a Plan
1. Navigate to **Settings > Organization > Billing**.
2. Select your desired plan from the **Upgrade** section.
3. Enter your payment details and click **Confirm**.

### Managing Payment Methods
1. In the **Billing** page, go to **Payment Methods**.
2. Add or remove credit cards as needed.
3. Access your **Invoice History** to download PDF receipts.

## Usage Alerts

To prevent unexpected overages or service pauses:
- **80% Usage**: A notification is sent to organization admins.
- **100% Usage**: AI features are paused until the next cycle or manual credit purchase.

<Tip>
  You can set custom **Daily Spending Limits** in the Billing settings to further control your team's credit consumption.
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Plan Support**: All Billing features require **Organization Administrator** permissions.
</Note>
