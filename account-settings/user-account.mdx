---

Your user account controls your personal experience and security within the Prov AI platform.

---

## Profile Settings

1. Click your **Profile Icon** in the bottom-left sidebar.
2. Select **User Account**.
3. You can update the following:
   - **Display Name**: The name shown to your team members.
   - **Avatar**: Upload a custom profile picture.
   - **Email**: The email address used for login and notifications.

---

## Password & Security

### Change Password
1. In the **User Account** page, click the **Security** tab.
2. Enter your current password and define a new, strong password.
3. Click **Update Password**.

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
<Info>
  **Plan Support**: MFA is recommended for all users and required for **Enterprise Plan** organizations.
</Info>

- Go to **Security > Multi-Factor Authentication**.
- Follow the prompts to set up an authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy).

---

## Notification Preferences

Manage how and where you receive updates from Prov AI.

| Notification | Channels | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **System Updates**| Email, In-app | Maintenance and new feature announcements. |
| **Model Deployed** | Email, Slack | Alerts when a project owner deploys model changes. |
| **Comments** | Email, In-app | Notifications when a team member mentions you. |
| **Credits Low** | Email | Monthly credit usage alerts for administrators. |

---

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
  **Permanent Action**: Deleting your account will remove your access to all Prov AI organizations and projects. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

1. In the **Danger Zone** section of your account page, click **Delete Account**.
2. Confirm your intention by typing your password.
3. Click **Confirm Deletion**.
