---
title: "Project Language"
description: "Configuring the default language for your Prov AI project"
---

Prov AI supports multiple languages for querying and metadata generation. Setting a default language ensures consistency across your team's interactions and AI reasoning.

---

## Setting the Language

1. Navigate to **Settings → Project → General**.
2. Locate the **Default Language** dropdown menu.
3. Select your preferred language (e.g., English, Spanish, Japanese, etc.).
4. Click **Save Changes**.

<Tip>
  Changing the project language affects how the AI interprets questions and generates suggested descriptions, but does not translate your underlying database data.
</Tip>
