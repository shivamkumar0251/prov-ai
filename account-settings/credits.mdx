Prov AI operating on a **Credit-Based System** for AI-powered features such as asking questions, generating charts, and data synchronization.

---

## Credit Consumption

Every interaction with Prov AI's engine consumes credits based on the complexity of the task:

| Action | Credit Cost | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ask Question** | 1 Credit | Querying your data using natural language. |
| **Generate Chart** | 1 Credit | Visualizing results into a chart. |
| **Metadata Generation**| 1 Credit | Using AI to suggest column or model descriptions. |
| **Relationship Discovery**| 1 Credit | Automatically identifying table joins. |

---

## Plan Allowances

Each Prov AI plan includes a baseline number of credits per month.

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Overages |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Free** | 50 Credits | Not Available |
| **Starter** | 500 Credits | $0.20 per credit |
| **Business** | 5,000 Credits | Custom |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited* | N/A |

<Tip>
  Credits reset on the first day of your billing cycle. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.
</Tip>

---

## Monitoring Usage

Administrators can monitor usage in real-time:
1. Navigate to **Settings → Organization → Billing**.
2. View the **Usage Progress** bar to see credits consumed in the current cycle.
3. Access **Usage History** to see detailed logs of which users are consuming credits.

<Note>
  If your organization reaches its credit limit, AI-powered features will be temporarily paused until the next cycle or until additional credits are purchased.
</Note>
