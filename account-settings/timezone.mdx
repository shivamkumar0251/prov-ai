---

Timezone settings ensure that date and time calculations in your queries align with your organization's operational hours.

---

## Configuring Timezone

1. Navigate to **Settings → Project → General**.
2. Select your local timezone from the **Timezone** menu.
3. Click **Save**.

<Note>
  This setting is used by the Prov Engine to interpret relative date filters (e.g., "yesterday", "last week") and to display timestamps in Dashboards and Threads.
</Note>
