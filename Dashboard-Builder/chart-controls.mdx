While the AI automatically selects the best visualization for your data, **Chart Controls** allow users to manually override and adjust specific chart settings.

---

## Available Controls

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visualization Type" icon="shapes">
    Instantly swap between Bar, Line, Pie, Area, or Table views.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Axis Configuration" icon="arrows-up-down-left-right">
    Swap X and Y axes, or change groupings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sorting" icon="arrow-down-a-z">
    Sort measures in Ascending or Descending order.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Aggregation" icon="calculator">
    Change the math logic (e.g., from `SUM(Revenue)` to `AVG(Revenue)`).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Best Practices & Warnings

<Warning>
**Important Notes on Editing:**
- **Incorrect Chart Types:** Forcibly changing a time-series trend from a Line Chart to a Pie Chart will completely obscure the insight.
- **Aggregation Risks:** Changing an aggregation (like `SUM` to `COUNT`) fundamentally alters what the data means. Always check axes labels!
</Warning>

- Trust the AI's initial chart selection; it is optimized based on the data shape.
- Keep visualization adjustments simple to maintain clarity.
- Validate your changes by checking the data table view if you are uncertain.
