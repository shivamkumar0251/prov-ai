**Filters and Drilldowns** empower users to explore high-level dashboard data interactively, zooming in on granular details without needing to write new queries.

---

## Dashboard Filters

Global or chart-specific filters allow users to narrow down data to focus on specific segments in real time.

**Common Examples:**
- `Region = US`
- `Date Range = Q4 2024`
- `Customer Status = Active`

<Info>
Applying a global filter to a Dashboard will cascade the condition to every underlying SQL query, dynamically updating all charts simultaneously.
</Info>

---

## Data Drilldowns

Drilldowns allow users to click directly on a visualization segment to explore deeper hierarchical levels of data.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View High-Level Data">
    User views a map showing overall sales in the **US**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill into Region">
    User clicks on the **US** segment -> The chart drills down to show sales by **State**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill into Granularity">
    User clicks on **California** -> The chart updates to show sales by **City**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why Interactive Dashboards Matter

By combining Filters and Drilldowns, a single dashboard can serve the needs of a C-level executive (looking for high-level trends) and a frontline manager (drilling down into specific, actionable anomalies) without needing separate reports.
