While Prov AI excels at generating complete dashboards instantly, **Manual Editing** gives users absolute control over the final presentation, allowing them to tweak AI outputs or build entirely custom dashboards from a blank canvas.

---

## UI Editor Components

The Manual Editor provides a familiar, drag-and-drop workspace:

## Dashboard Customization

With manual editing, you have endless flexibility:

- **Drag-and-Drop Layout:** Move charts around the grid intuitively.
- **Resize Widgets:** Make critical KPI cards larger, or shrink supplementary tables.
- **Add / Remove:** Delete irrelevant charts generated by the AI, or pin saved charts from other analyses.
- **Customize Labels:** Override AI-generated titles and axis labels for business clarity.

## Best Practices

<Warning>
**Avoid Over-Customization:**
- **Clutter:** Cramming 15 charts onto a single screen reduces clarity to zero.
- **Misalignment:** Misaligned charts or disjointed sizes disrupt the user's reading flow.
</Warning>

- **Keep it Clean:** Use negative space wisely.
- **Group Logically:** Place related insights (e.g., all marketing metrics) adjacent to one another.
- **Responsive Design:** Remember that other users might view your dashboard on smaller laptop screens.
