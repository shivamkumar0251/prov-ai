Connect your Slack workspace to Prov AI to ask questions in natural language, generate charts, and view reasoning — all without leaving your Slack channel.

---

## Installation

1. Go to **Settings → Slack Integration**.
2. Click **Add to Slack** and complete the authorization process.
3. Once setup is complete, your Slack workspace name will appear on the integration page.

<Note>
  **Slack App Review**: The Prov AI Slack app is currently under review for the official Slack Marketplace. You may see a disclaimer about the app not being approved during this process.
</Note>

---

## Project & Channel Mapping

Project and channel mapping allows you to assign different Prov AI projects to different Slack channels to ensure contextually relevant answers.

### Mapping Steps
- **Set a Default Project**: This project will be used for any channel not explicitly mapped.
- **Map Specific Channels**: (Optional) Assign individual Slack channels to specific projects to restrict access or focus results.

---

## Using Prov AI in Slack

### Asking Questions
1. **Mention**: `@Prov AI` in any mapped channel.
2. **Direct Message**: Send a DM to Prov AI without mentioning.
3. **Language**: Ask in any language; Prov AI responds in a thread.

### AI Capabilities

| Action | Command / Usage |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Follow-up** | Continue the conversation in the same thread. |
| **Generate Chart**| Type `Generate chart` as a follow-up message. |
| **See Reasoning** | Use the "See reasoning" button to view SQL logic. |
