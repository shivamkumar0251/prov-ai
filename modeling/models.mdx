**Models** are the fundamental unit in Prov AI. They combine schemas, metadata, relationships, and calculated fields from your data source to help both people and the AI understand your data.

---

## Soft Limitation

Each Prov AI project supports up to **200 models** by default. This is a soft limit; if your organization requires more, please contact our support team.

---

## Creating a Model

### 1. During Connection
When connecting a new data source, you'll be asked to select specific tables. These tables are automatically converted into data models.

### 2. From the Modeling Page
1. Click the **+** icon next to the **Models** section in the sidebar.
2. Select a table from your data source.
3. Choose the columns you want to include and move them to the "active" list using the **>** button.
4. Select a **Primary Key** (optional but recommended).
5. Click **Submit**.

<Tip>
  Prov AI allows you to connect only the necessary tables. You can always add or remove tables in Project Settings later.
</Tip>

---

## Managing Models

### Update Columns
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** on a model diagram.
2. Select **Update Columns**.
3. Add or remove columns to sync with the Prov Engine.

### Update Primary Key
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** on the model diagram.
2. Select **Update Primary Key**.

### Delete a Model
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** on the model diagram.
2. Select **Delete**.

---

## Model Lists & Navigation

Access your full list of models in the sidebar:
- **Expand**: Click the icon to see column details.
- **Focus**: Click a model name to jump to its position on the Entity Relationship diagram.

---

## Important: Deploying Changes

If you make modifications, an **Undeployed changes** notice will appear at the bottom of the page. 

1. Click **Update Models Now** to sync with the Prov Engine.
2. Status will show **Deploying...** and then **Synced**.

<Note>
  Project owners are automatically notified via the dashboard once model updates are completed.
</Note>
