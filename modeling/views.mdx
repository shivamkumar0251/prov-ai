---

**Views** are virtual tables that present data from underlying models. In Prov AI, the "View" feature specifically represents **"Trusted Results"** — saved SQL statements that provide consistent, non-regenerated data.

---

## How it Works

When you save an answer as a view:
- The question and its corresponding SQL are stored.
- When the same question is asked later, Prov AI uses the saved view immediately instead of using the LLM to regenerate SQL.
- This ensures **consistent answers** and **faster results**.

---

## Creating a View

Currently, views are created via the **Save as View** option on the **Home** page:

1. Ask a question and get an answer.
2. Click the **Save as View** button in the answer area.
3. Provide a name for the view.

---

## Managing Views

### View List
Access your views in the **Views** section of the Modeling sidebar:
- **Expand**: See column details.
- **Focus**: Click the name to jump to the view block on the ER diagram.

### Preview Metadata
Click a view diagram to see:
- **SQL Statement**: The exact SQL stored for this view.
- **Preview Data**: View up to **100 rows** of the result set.

### Delete a View
1. Click the **More icon (⋮)** on the view diagram.
2. Choose **Delete**.

---

## Visual Distinction

On the Entity Relationship diagram:
- **Models** are represented in **Blue**.
- **Views** are represented in **Green**.

<Note>
  Deploy your changes after creating or deleting views to ensure the Prov Engine is updated.
</Note>
