Prov AI’s modeling layer provides a conceptual framework that defines data structure and relationships. This blueprint ensures that your data is organized efficiently to meet complex business requirements.

---

## Core Components

| Component | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Models** | The fundamental unit combining schema, metadata, and semantics from your tables. |
| **Views** | Virtual tables that present data retrieved from underlying models based on predefined queries. |
| **ER Diagram** | A visual map of your models and their logical connections (relationships). |

---

## Models vs. Views

**Models** are direct representations of your data source tables refined with Prov AI semantics. 

**Views** are "Trusted Results." When you save a result SQL statement as a view, Prov AI stores it as a reference. If a user asks a similar question later, the Prov Engine uses the saved view instead of regenerating SQL, ensuring consistent and rapid responses.

---

## Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD)

The Modeling page features a dynamic ERD that visualizes your entire data landscape:
- **Blue Blocks**: Represent **Models**.
- **Green Blocks**: Represent **Views**.
- **Lines**: Visualize relationships and data flow between items.

<Note>
  The ERD is the primary workspace for managing calculated fields, editing metadata, and defining new relationships.
</Note>
