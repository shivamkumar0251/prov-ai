Prov AI is a powerful GenBI (Generative BI) solution that allows you to query any database in natural language, generates accurate SQL, dashboards, charts, and AI-powered insights in seconds designed to streamline your data workflows and enhance decision-making capabilities. Whether you're a startup looking to get started quickly or an enterprise requiring advanced features and customization, Prov AI offers flexible deployment options to meet your needs.

Today, getting to an answer is often harder than getting the data. Teams lose a surprising amount of time just guiding others to the right dashboard, report, or metric definition—rather than generating insights and acting on them. As dashboards proliferate, even simple questions (“Which dashboard shows email performance?” or “Where do I analyze campaign spend?”) become a scavenger hunt. And when answers require navigating dozens of filters or writing SQL, non-technical users get blocked and end up waiting on the analytics team for extracts and custom queries, often with days to a week of turnaround. Prov AI exists to remove this friction by letting anyone ask questions in natural language and instantly get reliable, governed results.
